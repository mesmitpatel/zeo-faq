Creating a Route

What are different ways to make route?APP

To download a report for a particular trip, go to the History section & scroll down to the stop you want to download the report for. Click on the three dots menu and you will see a “Download Report” option. Click on that to download the report for that particular trip. For any particular trip, the report will contain detailed numbers of all the important statistical measures such as -

  • Serial Number
  • Address
  • Distance from start
  • Original ETA
